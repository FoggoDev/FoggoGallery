My story

At the age of 13, I discovered the software Source Filmmaker [a Valve animation software]. I created many animations based on FNAF. Later, around the age of 15, I discovered a YouTube channel named Nico [a YouTube channel that focuses on UE4 game development]. I loved his videos, so I decided to buy his UE4 course to try it out. At first, I wasn't a fan, but as time went by, I became more passionate. When I turned 16, I started working with Blender. Of course, I began by creating a 3D model of a donut, but as time went on, I was able to create more beautiful models. And so, I'm sharing everything with you here.
