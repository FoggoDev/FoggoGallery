My Story

At the age of 13, I discovered Source Filmmaker [an animation software from Valve] and made a lot of animations based on FNAF. Later, when I was about 15, I discovered a YouTube channel called Nico [a YouTube channel dedicated to UE4 game development]. I loved his videos, so I decided to buy his UE4 course to try it out. At the beginning, I wasn't a big fan, but as time went by, I became more and more passionate about it. It was at the age of 16 that I started to work with Blender. Of course, I started by making a 3D model of a doughnut, but as time went on I was able to make more and more beautiful models.

Softwares I Use
