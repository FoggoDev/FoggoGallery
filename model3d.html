Here are some animatronic models that were created for the cancelled game Nightmare Night. We have Foggo, a blue fox, who can open his face in two parts. Then, there's Welpy, an ice cream-selling rabbit, who can open his face in six parts. Additionally, we have Foxou, a basic fox, who can open his face in six parts and his torso in two parts. And let's not forget Endo, the hidden base underneath all the animatronics.These models were developed between 2022 and 2023.
